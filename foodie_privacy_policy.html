最后更新日期：2026年5月20日

欢迎使用 Foodie Dice（以下简称"本应用"）。我们非常重视您的隐私保护和个人信息安全。本隐私政策旨在向您说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息。

1. 信息收集

我们收集的信息类型包括：

  • 设备信息：设备型号、操作系统版本、唯一设备标识符（IDFV）等，用于提供个性化服务和广告投放。
  • 使用数据：您在本应用中的操作记录、偏好设置、食物选择历史等，用于改善用户体验。
  • 广告标识符：用于展示个性化广告的标识符（如果您同意接收个性化广告）。

重要提示：我们不会收集您的姓名、电话号码、电子邮件地址、位置信息等个人身份信息。

2. 信息使用

我们收集的信息将用于以下目的：

  • 提供和改进本应用的功能和服务
  • 展示相关广告（通过 AdMob 广告服务）
  • 分析用户使用习惯，优化产品体验
  • 发送通知提醒（仅在您授权的情况下）
  • 防止欺诈和滥用行为

3. 第三方服务

本应用使用以下第三方服务：

  • Google AdMob：用于展示广告。AdMob 可能会收集您的设备信息和广告标识符以提供个性化广告。您可以选择在设备设置中限制广告跟踪。
  • Apple Store / Google Play：用于应用分发和内购服务，这些平台有自己的隐私政策。

我们建议您阅读这些第三方服务的隐私政策，了解他们如何处理您的信息。

4. 数据存储和安全

我们采取合理的技术和管理措施来保护您的信息安全：

  • 所有用户数据均存储在您的本地设备上
  • 我们不会将您的个人数据传输到远程服务器
  • 使用加密技术保护数据传输（如适用）
  • 定期审查和更新我们的安全措施

5. 您的权利

根据相关法律法规，您享有以下权利：

  • 访问权：您可以查看我们收集的关于您的信息
  • 删除权：您可以通过卸载应用或删除应用数据来清除所有本地存储的信息
  • 选择退出权：您可以随时在设备设置中关闭通知权限或限制广告跟踪
  • 撤回同意权：您可以随时撤回对我们收集和使用您信息的同意

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不专门面向13岁以下的儿童。如果我们发现无意中收集了儿童的个人信息，我们将尽快删除这些信息。

7. 隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何重大变更都会通过应用内通知或在我们的网站上公布。我们鼓励您定期查看本隐私政策以了解最新信息。

Privacy Policy

Foodie Dice Application

Last Updated: May 20, 2026

Welcome to Foodie Dice (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We take your privacy protection and personal information security very seriously. This Privacy Policy is designed to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection

The types of information we collect include:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifier (IDFV), etc., used to provide personalized services and advertising.
  • Usage Data: Your operation records, preference settings, food selection history in the App, used to improve user experience.
  • Advertising Identifiers: Identifiers used to display personalized ads (if you agree to receive personalized ads).

Important Note: We do NOT collect your personally identifiable information such as name, phone number, email address, or location information.

2. Information Use

The information we collect will be used for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving the App's features and services
  • Displaying relevant advertisements (through AdMob advertising service)
  • Analyzing user usage patterns to optimize product experience
  • Sending notification reminders (only with your authorization)
  • Preventing fraud and abuse

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect your device information and advertising identifiers to provide personalized ads. You can choose to limit ad tracking in your device settings.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: Used for app distribution and in-app purchase services. These platforms have their own privacy policies.

We recommend that you read the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand how they handle your information.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your information security:

  • All user data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not transmit your personal data to remote servers
  • Encryption technology is used to protect data transmission (where applicable)
  • Regular review and update of our security measures

5. Your Rights

According to relevant laws and regulations, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can view the information we have collected about you
  • Right to Delete: You can clear all locally stored information by uninstalling the App or deleting App data
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can turn off notification permissions or limit ad tracking in your device settings at any time
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent to our collection and use of your information at any time

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under 13 years of age. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from children, we will delete such information as soon as possible.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any material changes will be notified through in-app notifications or posted on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed.

8. Contact Us

Email: mement_support@163.com
