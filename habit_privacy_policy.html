隐私政策

欢迎使用燃习（Ember Habit）！我们非常重视您的隐私保护。本隐私政策将帮助您了解我们如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息。

1. 信息收集

我们仅收集为提供服务所必需的最少信息：

  • 习惯数据：您创建的习惯名称、图标、打卡记录等，这些数据仅存储在您的设备本地
  • 设置偏好：您的语言选择、振动/音效开关等应用设置
  • 设备信息：为确保应用正常运行，我们可能会收集设备型号、操作系统版本等技术信息

重要说明：所有个人数据均存储在您的设备本地，我们不会上传或收集您的任何个人信息到服务器。

2. 信息使用

我们收集的信息仅用于：

  • 提供和改进习惯追踪功能
  • 保存您的个人设置和偏好
  • 展示统计数据和趋势分析
  • 确保应用的稳定性和性能

3. 信息共享

我们不会与任何第三方共享、出售或出租您的个人信息。

所有数据都存储在您的设备上，只有您可以访问这些数据。

4. 数据安全

我们采取合理的技术措施保护您的数据安全：

  • 使用SQLite本地数据库存储数据
  • 数据仅在您的设备上存储和处理
  • 不通过网络传输任何个人数据

5. 广告与解锁

作为免费用户，您可以通过观看广告来解锁额外的习惯位：

  • 广告由第三方广告平台提供
  • 我们不会收集您的广告交互数据
  • 您可以选择不观看广告，此时可使用基础的3个习惯位

6. 儿童隐私

我们的应用适合所有年龄段的用户。我们不专门针对13岁以下儿童收集个人信息。

7. 您的权利

对于存储在您设备上的数据，您拥有完全的控制权：

  • 随时查看、编辑或删除您的习惯数据
  • 清除应用数据以删除所有本地存储的信息
  • 卸载应用以彻底删除所有数据

8. 隐私政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。重大变更时，我们会通过应用内通知告知您。

9. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过以下方式联系我们：

Privacy Policy

Welcome to Ember Habit (燃习)! We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy will help you understand how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information Collection

We only collect the minimum information necessary to provide our services:

  • Habit Data: Habit names, icons, check-in records you create, all stored locally on your device
  • Settings Preferences: Your language selection, vibration/sound toggle settings
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version and other technical information to ensure proper app functionality

Important Note: All personal data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or collect any of your personal information to servers.

2. Information Use

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Providing and improving habit tracking features
  • Saving your personal settings and preferences
  • Displaying statistics and trend analysis
  • Ensuring app stability and performance

3. Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third parties.

All data is stored on your device, and only you can access it.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data:

  • Using SQLite local database for data storage
  • Data is only stored and processed on your device
  • No personal data is transmitted over the network

5. Advertising & Unlocking

As a free user, you can watch ads to unlock additional habit slots:

  • Ads are provided by third-party advertising platforms
  • We do not collect your ad interaction data
  • You can choose not to watch ads and use the basic 3 habit slots

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not specifically collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have complete control over data stored on your device:

  • View, edit, or delete your habit data at any time
  • Clear app data to delete all locally stored information
  • Uninstall the app to completely remove all data

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: mement_support@163.com
