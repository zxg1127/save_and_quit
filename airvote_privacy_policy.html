AirVote 隐私政策

最后更新日期：2026年7月4日

核心要点：AirVote 是一款完全离线的投票工具，我们不会收集您的任何个人数据，所有数据仅在您的设备本地处理。

1. 引言

欢迎使用 AirVote（以下简称"本应用"）。我们深知隐私对您的重要性，并郑重承诺保护您的隐私。本隐私政策将向您说明我们如何处理您的信息。

由于本应用的离线特性，我们不会收集、存储、传输或分享您的任何个人信息。请您仔细阅读本政策。

2. 我们收集的信息

2.1 个人信息

我们不收集任何可识别个人身份的信息，包括但不限于：

  • 姓名、手机号、邮箱地址等联系方式
  • 地理位置信息
  • 通讯录、相册、相机等敏感权限数据
  • 设备唯一标识符（如 IMEI、MAC 地址）

2.2 投票数据

您创建或参与的投票数据仅存储在您的设备本地，通过蓝牙（BLE）在设备间直接传输，不会上传到任何服务器。

2.3 设备标识符

为了实现蓝牙设备发现和投票防重复提交功能，本应用会使用随机生成的设备 ID。该 ID 仅存储在本地设备上，不会与任何个人信息关联，也不会上传到服务器。

3. 我们如何使用信息

由于我们不收集您的个人信息，因此也不会以任何方式使用您的个人信息。

本地存储的投票数据仅用于：

  • 在您的设备上显示投票内容和结果
  • 通过蓝牙与附近设备同步投票数据
  • 防止同一设备重复投票

4. 信息共享与披露

我们不会将您的任何信息出售、出租或分享给第三方。

投票数据仅通过蓝牙在参与投票的设备之间直接传输，不经过任何中间服务器。

5. 数据存储与安全

  • 所有数据仅存储在您的设备本地
  • 您可以随时在应用内删除投票数据
  • 卸载应用将清除所有本地数据
  • 蓝牙传输数据仅在设备通信范围内有效

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 周岁以下的儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。由于本应用不收集任何个人信息，因此也不存在儿童隐私问题。

7. 权限说明

7.1 蓝牙权限

用于发现附近设备并传输投票数据。这是本应用的核心功能所需权限。

7.2 位置权限（部分 Android 版本）

在部分 Android 版本中，蓝牙扫描需要位置权限。我们不会收集或使用您的位置信息。

7.3 通知权限

用于在收到新投票或投票状态更新时发送通知。您可以在系统设置中关闭此权限。

8. 应用内购买

本应用可能包含应用内购买功能。购买交易由 Apple App Store 或 Google Play 处理，我们不会收集您的支付信息。

9. 隐私政策的更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在本页面发布，并注明更新日期。

10. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过以下方式联系我们：

Key Point: AirVote is a fully offline voting tool. We do not collect any of your personal data. All data is processed locally on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to AirVote (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We understand the importance of privacy to you and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Due to the offline nature of the App, we do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information. Please read this policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • Name, phone number, email address, or other contact information
  • Geographic location information
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or other sensitive permission data
  • Device unique identifiers (such as IMEI, MAC address)

2.2 Voting Data

Votes you create or participate in are stored only locally on your device and transmitted directly between devices via Bluetooth (BLE). No data is uploaded to any server.

2.3 Device Identifier

To enable Bluetooth device discovery and prevent duplicate vote submissions, the App uses a randomly generated device ID. This ID is stored only on the local device, is not associated with any personal information, and is not uploaded to any server.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect your personal information, we do not use your personal information in any way.

Locally stored voting data is only used for:

  • Displaying vote content and results on your device
  • Synchronizing vote data with nearby devices via Bluetooth
  • Preventing duplicate votes from the same device

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share any of your information with third parties.

Voting data is transmitted directly between participating devices via Bluetooth, without going through any intermediate servers.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored only locally on your device
  • You can delete voting data within the app at any time
  • Uninstalling the app will clear all local data
  • Bluetooth data transmission is only effective within device communication range

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App does not collect any personal information, there are no children's privacy concerns.

7. Permission Explanations

7.1 Bluetooth Permission

Used to discover nearby devices and transmit voting data. This is a core function permission required by the App.

7.2 Location Permission (Some Android Versions)

On some Android versions, Bluetooth scanning requires location permission. We do not collect or use your location information.

7.3 Notification Permission

Used to send notifications when new votes are received or vote status updates occur. You can disable this permission in system settings.

8. In-App Purchases

The App may include in-app purchase functionality. Purchase transactions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not collect your payment information.

9. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page with the update date noted.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: mement_support@163.com
