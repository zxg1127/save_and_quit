Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 7, 2026

TalkTune (言犀) (hereinafter referred to as "we", "our", or "TalkTune") highly values the protection of users' privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy is intended to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information, as well as your related rights.

Please read and understand this Privacy Policy carefully before using our services. If you disagree with any part of this Privacy Policy, please stop using TalkTune services immediately.

1. Information Collection and Use

1.1 Local Data Processing

TalkTune is a chat analysis application based on on-device AI. We commit that:

  • All chat data processing is performed locally on your device and will not be uploaded to any external servers;
  • We do not collect, store, or access your chat record content;
  • All functions such as OCR text recognition, message classification, and analysis result generation are executed locally.

1.2 Locally Stored Data

To provide a better user experience, we store the following information locally on your device:

  • Analysis history records: Including analysis time, relationship type, sentiment scores, and other metadata;
  • User preference settings: Such as language settings, interface preferences, etc.;
  • AI model files: Stored in the application's private sandbox directory.

These data are stored only on your device, and you can delete this information at any time by clearing application data.

1.3 Permission Requests

To enable related features, TalkTune may request the following permissions:

  • Photo/Album access: Used to select chat screenshots for analysis;
  • Camera access: Used to capture chat screenshots;
  • Network access: Only used to download AI model files.

2. Data Security

We use industry-standard security technologies and measures to protect your data:

  • All data processing is performed locally on your device without your permission to transmit to any external servers;
  • Application data is stored in an environment protected by the operating system sandbox;
  • We regularly update the application to fix potential security vulnerabilities.

3. Third-Party Services

TalkTune may use third-party services in the following scenarios:

  • AI model downloads: Downloading open-source AI model files from third-party servers;
  • In-app purchases: Processing subscriptions and purchases through the Apple App Store.

These third-party services have their own independent privacy policies. We recommend that you read and understand the relevant terms before using them.

4. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access: You can view locally stored application data at any time;
  • Right to delete: You can delete relevant data by clearing application data or using the delete function within the application;
  • Right to refuse: You can refuse to provide non-essential permissions, but this may affect the use of certain features.

5. Minors' Privacy

We attach great importance to the protection of minors' privacy. If you are a minor under 18 years old, please read and understand this Privacy Policy with the company of your guardian, and use our services only with the consent and guidance of your guardian.

6. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated Privacy Policy will be published in a prominent location within the application. We recommend that you review it regularly. If we make significant changes to the Privacy Policy, we will notify you through application notifications or other means.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us through the following methods:

Email：mement_support@163.com

We will reply to your inquiry as soon as possible.
